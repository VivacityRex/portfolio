Who is Vivacity?

Well - I am Vivacity, an individual on the autism spectrum who has embarked on a remarkable journey of self-discovery and personal growth. My educational path took a different turn when I left public school in grade 9 due to being undiagnosed at the time. The traditional classroom environment posed significant challenges for me, and it became clear that I needed an alternative approach to learning.

As an adult, I embarked on a quest to understand myself better and realized that my love for learning and education could be pursued outside the confines of a conventional classroom. I embraced online learning to complete my high school education, allowing me to navigate the academic landscape in a way that suited my unique needs and learning style.

Eager to expand my knowledge and skills, I pursued upgrading classes from college, seeking to fill any gaps in my education. This dedication and commitment led me to achieve a diploma in Accounting, a testament to my perseverance and determination.

However, my thirst for knowledge and curiosity knew no bounds. It was during this time that I discovered my true passion ‚Äì coding. I found solace and excitement in the world of programming, where creativity and problem-solving converge. I became enamored with the endless possibilities and the opportunity to continuously learn and grow in this field.

Driven by my love for learning and the desire to make up for lost time, I fully immersed myself in the world of coding. I eagerly absorbed as much knowledge as possible, honing my skills and expanding my expertise. Each new concept and programming language became a stepping stone on my path to mastery.

My story is one of resilience, determination, and an unwavering dedication to personal and professional development. By embracing a non-traditional educational path, I have discovered the power of self-directed learning and the transformative impact it can have on one's life. My journey serves as a testament to the importance of adaptability, a growth mindset, and the pursuit of one's passions.
